About Us

SLIET Software Development Club (SSDC), is a Departmental Club of Computer Science & Engineering, SLIET Longowal. This club was founded by CSE - 2017 undergrads. The team currently comprises of 40+ developers. The team aims to brush up the coding skills in young enthusiasts, with major goals to crack the latest Coding Contests, Challenges & Interviews without any reluctance landing them in some decent technical job prospects. In due course of grooming it's pupils, the club focuses on project-oriented approach in diverse fields (Web Development, App Development, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, etc) to ensure holistic development of it's members. Furthermore, the club organises workshops and monthly coding compitions for college students as well which in turn helps us discover young wizards in IT fields.

Our Faculty Advisor

"Life is an ever changing process like versions of software. If thou don't keep updated, thou may be depleted."

It's the age of Industry 4.0, where the world is developing on the grounds of interconnectivity, automation, machine learning, artificial intelligence, real time data, etc. It's the phase that sometimes referred to as IIoT or smart manufacturing, marries physical production and operations with smart digital technology, machine learning, and big data to create a more holistic and better connected ecosystem for companies that focus on manufacturing and supply chain management. While every company and organization adopting these new technologies, it becomes inevitable for recruiters to seek such tech stacks in our enthusiastic engineers. So, we at SSDC, aim to impart the latest trends in information technologies to our students and make them future ready. The club disseminates various coding practices, algorithms, optimization techniques, fields of study, etc. to it's members in order to help them face tough challenges ahead. With a noble motive to share programming knowledge among others in the vicinity & build a wholesome coding ecosystem. The club organises various competitions at major coding platforms and regularly posts challenges in our social media handles. With every growing year, the club emphasizes to introduce something new that could supplement it's members to increase their outreach. Things like participating in renowned hackathons & coding competitions remains a must, which thereby offers opportunities to explore their knowledge depths as well.
It's rightly said, you no longer become a great opportunist until you explore the length and depth of the subject matter, & so is practiced at our club. So instead of sitting idle, timelessly contemplating on doubts and being frustrated with the errors, come join us and get curated path to success. And most importantly,

"The best way to predict the future is to implement it.”
— David Heinemeier Hansson
